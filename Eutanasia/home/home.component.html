Globe Guides

At Globe Guides, we create experiences for you to take your team beyond the ordinary.

We believe that by providing people with transformative experiences, we enable them to grow exponentially and connect to the world in a fresh way. They return to the office with a new spark in their step, ready to embrace work and uniquely equipped to tackle challenges.

Whatever your passion, we’ll help you bring it to life so you can vitalize your team, change their world and energize your business. We focus on the details so you can focus on the big picture.

Services

Globe Guides will be your best ally. Our services focus on your needs and those of your work team, so that, with the experiences, your company can grow in ideas and new business opportunities.

Incentive Travel

Motivating your team is important. Today’s employees want more than tokens, they want ownership of their successes and experiences they can share. Give them something to take personal, an incentive trip to look forward to and the opportunity to share that. Take care of your team and watch your business.

When you want to take care your team, the location isn’t the important part. What matters is to get them a real taste of the world you want them to conquer, giving them the perfect environment to clear their mind and build new ideas for a new level in their lives and business.

Team Retreats

Efficiency and success is when all part work together for the same result. Your team is no different. Create opportunities for them to work together outside of the office, where they can show leadership, where they can relax a little and where they can see you have fun.

Leadership is about inspiring those around us, create that opportunity for others to see you as the one inspiring them and giving them the opportunity to step into their own.
